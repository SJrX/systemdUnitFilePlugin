Check whether given cgroup controllers (e.g. cpu) are available for use on the system.

Multiple controllers may be passed with a space separating them; in this case the condition will only pass if all listed controllers are available for use. Controllers unknown to systemd are ignored. Valid controllers are cpu, cpuset, io, memory, and pids. Even if available in the kernel, a particular controller may not be available if it was disabled on the kernel command line with cgroup_disable=controller.
